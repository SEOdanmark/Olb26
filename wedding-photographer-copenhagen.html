Frequently Asked Questions

How many photos should a photographer give for a wedding?

A photographer should provide a sufficient number of edited photos to capture all the significant moments and details of the wedding day. This often ranges from a few hundred to a thousand or more, depending on the package and agreement with the couple.

How long before a wedding should you get a photographer?

It's recommended to book a wedding photographer as soon as possible, ideally at least 6 to 12 months before the wedding date, especially if you have specific preferences or if the photographer is in high demand.

How many photos should I get back from my wedding?

The number of photos returned to a couple can vary depending on factors such as the length of coverage, the photographer's shooting style, and the events of the day. Couples can typically expect to receive several hundred edited photos.
